Overview

Purpose

We intend to be a gateway to adventure seekers, by connecting you to a thrilling adventure on the water. Here you will find a place to find resources to package the perfect trip by giving access to itineraries, rafting locations and connecting you to professional guides.

Audience

Who are the target customers?

Our target customer is varying ages averaging from college to middle age who are seeking adventure.

How will my audience usually access my site?

Customers will access the site by desktop, laptop, and mobile devices.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?

They will be able to research destinations, build a travel itinerary, find pricing, and choose a guide.
