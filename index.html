Utah

Utah is in the West and bordered by Idaho, Wyoming, Colorado and Arizona. It is a state of so many landscapes. Geography ranges and includes, mountains, deserts, red rocks and lakes. It is a tourist destination for skiing, jeeping, red rock climbing, and setting landspeed records on the salt flats.
